Pizza

Description

Read on for the world's best home-made pizza recipe

Note: I'm not like the biryani guy. I don't care about history.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Mix water, all-purpose flour, yeast and sugar
  2. Stick meat and cheese into the dough ball
  3. Bake it and shove it in your pie-hole
